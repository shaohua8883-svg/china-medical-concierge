How It Works

Our Step-by-Step Process

A single bilingual personal care manager handles every step. No handoffs, no portals, no guesswork.

Contact Us

Send us your medical records from anywhere in the world. Within days, you'll know whether China is the right option for your condition, and which department fits best.

  • Initial feasibility review of your records
  • Department and specialty match against our hospital network
  • Realistic timeline and cost ballpark in writing
  • No fee, no commitment

Your Medical Assessment

We match you to a named specialist at the right department. If you want a second opinion before flying, we set up a remote consult so you decide with full information.

  • Named specialist matched, not a generic department referral
  • Optional remote second opinion (video, with translator)
  • Treatment plan options laid out in plain English
  • You decide whether to proceed

Travel & Visa Support

Once you confirm, we lock everything in: appointments, hospital invitation letter for visa, flights guidance, hotel near the hospital, daily itinerary.

  • Confirmed appointments with the specialist team
  • Official hospital invitation letter for S1/S2 visa
  • Hotel booking near the hospital
  • Day-by-day itinerary delivered before departure

Arrival in China

Your personal care manager meets you at the airport. SIM card, hotel check-in, and a clear briefing on what happens tomorrow at the hospital. You're never figuring it out alone.

  • Airport pickup by your bilingual personal care manager
  • SIM card and local payment app setup
  • Hotel check-in and neighborhood orientation
  • Pre-visit briefing for your first hospital day

Hospital & Treatment

A bilingual personal care manager is with you for every hospital visit. Registration, doctor translation, payments, lab routing, pharmacy. You focus on the doctor; we handle everything else.

  • Same personal care manager every visit, no handoffs
  • Real-time medical translation with the specialist
  • Payments, lab routing, pharmacy pickup
  • Same-day notes summarized in English for you

Insurance & Follow-up

After you fly home, we package everything your home doctor needs and stay reachable for follow-up questions. The handoff is the part most agencies skip — it's the part we obsess over.

  • Bilingual medical records package (PDF + originals)
  • Treatment summary written for your home doctor
  • Itemized bills with English translation for insurance
  • Remote follow-up with the China specialist if needed
